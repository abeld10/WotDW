Game Instructions

Objective: Your goal is to guess the hidden word correctly. Each day, there's a new word to discover.

  • Daily Challenge: You have only one chance per day to be on the leaderboard. After your first game, the hidden word will be revealed to you.
  • You have as many tries as there are letters in the word to guess it correctly (example: 5 tries for a 5-letter word).
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
    • Tile Colors:
  • Green: The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Grey: The letter is not in the word.
  • Timer: There's a timer running as soon as you start the game. Try to guess the word as quickly as possible!
Please click "OK" to start the game and begin the timer. Good luck, and have fun!
